Chakrata Trip


I have spend 3 days there , I have seen mountains and green forest and feel the energy between those mountains.

  1. Taj Mahal – The iconic marble mausoleum of India.
  2. Qutub Minar – Famous for its ancient architecture and history.
  3. Red Fort – A historical fortress with stunning design.
  4. India Gate – A war memorial and popular sightseeing spot.
  5. Lotus Temple – Known for its unique flower-like design and tranquility.
  • Butter Chicken – Creamy and rich in flavor.
  • Chole Bhature – A classic North Indian breakfast dish.
  • Paneer Tikka – Deliciously marinated and grilled cottage cheese.
  • Gulab Jamun – Sweet milk-based dessert in syrup.
  • Biryani – Aromatic spiced rice with meat or vegetables.
