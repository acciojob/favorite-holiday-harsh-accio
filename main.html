Paris


Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its art, culture, and history. It is a city that beautifully combines romantic charm with modern vibrancy, offering countless experiences for travelers.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – A symbol of Paris and a must-see landmark.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the world-famous Mona Lisa and other masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – An architectural marvel with stunning Gothic design.
  4. Montmartre – Famous for its artistic vibe and hilltop views.
  5. Champs-Élysées – A shopping and cultural avenue loved by tourists.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant – A flaky and buttery French pastry.
  • Baguette – The classic French bread that pairs with everything.
  • Crème Brûlée – A rich and creamy dessert with caramelized sugar topping.
  • Escargot – Snails cooked with garlic and butter, a French delicacy.
  • Macarons – Colorful sweet almond cookies loved worldwide.
