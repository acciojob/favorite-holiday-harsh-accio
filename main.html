Chakrata Trip


I have spend 3 days there , I have seen mountains and green forest and feel the energy between those mountains.

  1. Taj Mahal – A marble wonder of the world.
  2. Red Fort – Known for its historical significance.
  3. Qutub Minar – Famous for its ancient architecture.
  4. Lotus Temple – A peaceful place for meditation.
  5. India Gate – A memorial to soldiers of India.
  • Butter Chicken – Rich and creamy.
  • Chole Bhature – A spicy and filling dish.
  • Paneer Tikka – Grilled cottage cheese delicacy.
  • Biryani – Aromatic rice with spices.
  • Gulab Jamun – Sweet milk-based dessert.
